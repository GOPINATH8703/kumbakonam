Kumbakonam – Temple Heritage

Kumbakonam is a historic temple town in Tamil Nadu with numerous temples dedicated to both Lord Vishnu and Lord Shiva, including the Adi Kumbeswarar Temple, Sarangapani Temple, and Airavatesvara Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site. It is also a center for the nine Navagraha temples, each dedicated to one of the nine planets.
